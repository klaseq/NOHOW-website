Home

welcome to the website of the band NOHOW

The band Nohow comes from Uhersky Brod and plays punk pop. The band
has been on the local and Slovak scene for more than 15 years.

History

The band was formed in Uhersky Brod and originally had 5 members. Later the number of members was reduced to 4.
Then the guitarist left and the band continued with 3 members. The current line-up has
again 4 members, two guitars, bass and drums.
